Senior researcher at CERMICS and professor at Ecole des Ponts. My work focuses on the mathematical and numerical analysis of models from molecular simulation, with a current emphasis on computational statistical physics. This involves, from a mathematical viewpoint, techniques ranging from probability theory and the study of stochastic processes, to functional analysis and the theory of partial differential equations; as well as numerical analysis, scientific computing and machine learning.
